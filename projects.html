  • Permulin
  • Permulin is centred on the idea of providing both personal and shared in- and output for collaborating users.
  • EarPut
  • EarPut is a novel interface concept, which instruments the ear as an interactive surface for touch-based interactions and its prototypical hardware implementation.
  • Collaborative TV
  • We propose a number of interaction techniques allowing TV viewers to use their mobile phones to view and share content with others in the room, thus supporting local social interaction.
  • PaperVideo
  • In this project, we investigated how interaction with video can benefit from multiple paper-like displays that support interaction with motion and sound.
  • Xpaaand
  • Future generations of displays will be thin, lighweight, and flexible. In this project, we develop novel interaction techniques for displays which can be dynamically expanded and collapsed.
  • FoldMe
  • In this project, we present a novel device concept that features double-sided displays which can be folded using predefined hinges.
  • Wipe’n’Watch
  • In this project, we investigate how novel mobile user interfaces can be utilized to support users efficiently when working with videos on the move.
